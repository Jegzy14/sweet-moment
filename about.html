Our History

Superior service and personalized attention

We're proud to offer the highest quality, freshest diner food on The Main Line. From our family to yours, we put lots of love and careful attention in each item. We hope you enjoy our work as much as we enjoy bringing it to you. Sweet Moment offers an extensive menu with everything imaginable at a diner, including salads, soups, soft drinks, desserts, breakfast foods, jollof rice, fried rice, fried yam, fried potato, fried plaintain, vegetable yam porridge, hot and cold sandwiches, burgers, spaghetti meatball, steamed peppered beef, steamed peppered chicken, cooked beans, beans porridge, and nigeria ramen. All baking and cooking is done on the premises, and transfered are accepted once it is confirmed. We also offer a full bar menu. It's our honor to serve you. Welcome and enjoy!
